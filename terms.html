2. Strict No-Refund Policy

Due to the custom nature of our products, all sales are final. Once an order has been approved and moved to production, we incur costs for materials, labor, and machine time that cannot be recovered.

Orders may only be cancelled before the final proof approval. Once you have approved the design/proof and production has started, the order cannot be cancelled or refunded.

The client is responsible for submitting high-resolution files suitable for printing. Madol Press is not responsible for poor print quality resulting from low-resolution or poor-quality files provided by the client.
